About CloudTunes

What is CloudTunes?

CloudTunes is our (Jason Lin and Calvin Lin) project for Hack the North 2019. We decided to make an app that allows users to enter a city and have all the weather information for that city displayed for the user. The app also plays music/sounds that matches that weather as well. The atmosphere created by this is meant to allow the person to have an immersive experience while checking the weather. This app is our attempt to learn new ways of programming as we are experimenting with new languages and ideas. The method behind the app is quite simple as well. Once the user has input a city, our app makes an API call to obtain all the necessary weather information for that specific city. The rest is just displaying it to the screen. Thank you for taking a look at our project.
